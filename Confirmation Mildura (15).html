You are now confirmed to attend Optimum Patient Care's 2017 Educational Workshop, where you will have the chance to interact with international and local experts in asthma and rhinitis to improve respiratory care, using real life case studies.

Please note that free street parking is available on Eighth Street and Orange Avenue.

GPs will be joined by world leading clinical experts, our collaborators at The Woolcock Institute of Medical Research and representatives from our event sponsor, Mylan.

For those who choose to participate, GPs will be supported to undertake a clinical review program in their practices using patient questionnaires and clinical data. Our multi-disciplinary team will assist GPs with the development of the Asthma Cycle of Care for patients. Patient privacy will be respected at all times. No identifiable data is removed from the GP practice.

 
What to Expect:
 
Opportunity to participate within a clinical audit activity post educational meeting and obtain 40 Category 1 CPD points (subject to approval from the RACGP)
 
Liaising with respiratory specialists
 
Support with GP Management Plans
 
Support with Asthma Cycle of Care
 
Opportunities for research collaboration
 
Dinner provided complimentary
